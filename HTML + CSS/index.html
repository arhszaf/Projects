What is MOOC Education?

MOOC Education is an MOOC platform that provides online courses.In addition to traditional course materials, such as filmed lectures, readings, and problem sets, MOOC Education provides interactive courses with user forums or social media discussions to support community interactions among students, professors, and teaching assistants (TAs), as well as immediate feedback to quick quizzes and assignments.

Who is this website aimed for?

MOOC Education is aimed for student looking to learn new skills or even adults looking for a career relative to some of the courses offered

How do i start?

Head over to the Subject section of the page and look for a subject that interests you. You can check out what Courses are offered for each Subject. if you have any questions feel free to contact us.
